Our Story

Founded in 2020, SK AI Hub has been at the forefront of AI education in India. Our mission is to democratize AI knowledge and make it accessible to everyone, from beginners to professionals looking to upskill.

We believe that AI is not just a technology—it's the future of work, innovation, and problem-solving. Our comprehensive curriculum covers everything from Python programming to advanced deep learning techniques.
